Dosa

Description

A dosa is a popular South Indian dish, usually enjoyed with sambhar and coconut chutney. It is made from a fermented batter of rice flour and urad dal.

Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Urad Dal
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the urad dal and soak it in water.
  2. Add 2 cups of water to the rice flour and whisk it. Let the rice flour soak for at least 1 hour.
  3. Drain the excess water from the urad dal and grind it without adding water.
  4. Now add water and grind the urad dal to a smooth batter.
  5. Add the soaked rice flour along with water and salt to the batter, and grind the batter again.
  6. Leave the batter overnight or at least for 12 hours.
  7. Now, the batter would have raised well. Mix the batter well and add some water if it is too thick.
  8. Heat a pan and pour a ladleful of batter in the pan.
  9. Spread the batter thinly and uniformly.
  10. Let the dosa cook till you see it turning golden brown from the bottom.
  11. Drizzle one or two tsp of oil all over the dosa.
  12. Let the dosa turn crispy. Once done, fold the dosa and serve it with sambhar and your favourite chutney.

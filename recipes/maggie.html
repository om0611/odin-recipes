Maggie

Description

Maggie noodles are very tasty, and can be made very quickly and easily.

Ingredients

  • Maggie 2-Minute Noodles Packets

Steps

  1. Pour water in a cooking pot and let it boil.
  2. Meanwhile, cut open the maggie packets and the masala packets that come inside the maggie packets.
  3. Pour the masala in the cooking pot and mix it.
  4. Now, put the noodle cake in the cooking pot and break it into smaller chunks.
  5. Stir until most of the water is boiled off. You are done!
